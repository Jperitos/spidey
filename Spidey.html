WHO IS SPIDERMAN?

Spider-Man is the kind of character that has something for everyone to love. There are so many facets to the characters that make him one of the greats, both in-universe and for readers in the real world.His compassion is challenged in the worst way with the death of Aunt May, and he teeters on the edge of succumbing to a darkness that embodies a lot of superheroes in modern times in the movies and in the comics. But he ultimately proves his integrity and humanity and in doing so, the movie becomes one of the best superhero stories ever put on film.
